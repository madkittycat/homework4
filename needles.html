Essential knitting tools

Knitting needles

Aluminum

Aluminum needles are the most widely used knitting needles and are much stronger than any of the other materials. These needles are perfect for all types of thread. These feather-light knitting needles with their conical tips are particularly suitable for working with fine wool. Due to the smooth surface, the stitches slide automatically from one needle to the next.

Wooden

Knitting needles made of wood are suitable for everyone who loves the warm feeling of the material in their hands and doesnt want to make any noises while knitting.

Bamboo

Bamboo is particularly popular with newbies, these knitting needles are not only cheap, but also pleasant to use. The smooth surface allows easy knitting and so learning the art is particularly easy for beginners.
